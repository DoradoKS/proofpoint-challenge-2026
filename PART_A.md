2. Describe a situation you had to resolve with a teammate from a study or work group.

After presenting our final group project (a Clinical Management System in Python), our team received feedback on architectural flaws. We needed to implement the Singleton Pattern to centralize database access (avoiding concurrency locks), add email notifications via SMTP, and correct session management without affecting the existing codebase. This refactoring phase was challenging due to the risk of regressions in a project developed by multiple members.

As a result, we transformed a basic MVP into a robust application. The database connection problems disappeared, the system sent emails correctly in real time, and we received theme approval, demonstrating our ability to adapt to technical requirements as a team.
